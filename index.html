About Me

My name is CHARLES MAMBWE CHALWE, and I'm a passionate 4th-year Computer Science student at the University of Zambia, proudly majoring in Software Engineering. My journey into web development began when I built my first website in high school, and I've been captivated by the endless possibilities of creating for the web ever since.

I believe that great websites are not just visually appealing but also accessible, performant, and intuitive to use. My approach combines technical excellence with user-centered design principles to create digital experiences that make a difference. While I am currently building my skills in certain technologies, I have a strong affinity for phone applications and web designs.

The love and passion for my ability to solve problems creatively and build things that people can use and enjoy drives me forward. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, mentoring fellow students, or enjoying a cold passion lemonade while learning from web development tutorials. I'm also an avid reader of tech blogs and design publications.
